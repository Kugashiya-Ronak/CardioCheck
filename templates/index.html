Understanding Cardiovascular Disease

1

Global Impact

CVD causes approximately 17.9 million deaths globally every year, making it a leading cause of mortality.

2

Preventable Risk

Over 80% of premature heart disease and stroke can be prevented through lifestyle modifications and early detection.
